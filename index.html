Texas Funeral Carriage

Honoring Loved Ones With Timeless Elegance

Honor your loved one with the dignity and tradition of a horse-drawn funeral procession from Texas Funeral Carriage, part of the trusted White Horse and Carriage Company in San Antonio. Serving families across Texas, we provide a full range of elegant funeral vehicles, including our white horse-drawn hearse, traditional black caisson, elegant family carriages, and the solemn riderless horse. Every detail is handled with care, ensuring a graceful, unforgettable tribute that celebrates a life with respect and timeless elegance.

Hearse

  • Elegant white carriage design for a graceful farewell
  • Perfect for honoring a loved one with timeless beauty and dignity
  • Handcrafted details and custom ironwork for a classic, refined look

Caisson

  • Traditional open design used for veterans and military services
  • Symbolizes honor, respect, and solemn remembrance
  • Ideal for formal processions and full military honors

Family Carriage

  • Comfortable, classic carriage for family participants in the procession
  • Complements either the white hearse or black caisson beautifully
  • Offers a peaceful and dignified way for loved ones to accompany the departed
